Welcome to the laboratory

This is where I tinker and experiment with different features before I make them live on my portfolio page, or a different project. I will also be using this page to showcase some of my old photography work. Please feel free to look around, or get in touch if you have any questions.

The Philippines

My final project. Photojournalism, and documentary style photography has always held a special place in my heart. In this project my goal was to capture 'The Filipino People'. I am still not sure what my full intent was going into this - I only knew I had one chance, and as my mentors had told me before I left "shoot, then shoot more,".
