Easy Recipeazy

Unsure of what to eat for dinner? Picky, or indecisive? Don't know what to make with what you have on hand? We have a solution. Easy Recipeazy is the application made for simplifying dinner, or a drink, or both. Users can simply enter in ingredients they have on hand, or preferences of what they would like to use, and our application will randomly show a meal or drink option (or both) depending on their inputs.
